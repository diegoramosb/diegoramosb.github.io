Projects

Throughout my degree, I've worked in several projects. The most remarkable ones are the following:

  • Cóndor: My and Mariana Moreno's final Bachelor's degree project. Cóndor is a tex-mining-based tool that facilitates the work of a communications analyst in the development of media impact reports. It allows the search of Twitter accounts of interest and the visualization of the results of content analysis of their tweets according to customized filters. The project aims to design the architecture that supports the application from the functionalities and graphics it provides. Additionally, Condor includes a model to automate the sentiment analysis of the tweets in the tool.
  • CookIA: A mobile cooking application developed in the Ionic framweork with an integrated voice assistant that is like Siri for cooking. This was the middle-of-degree project that I participated in.
    For this project, we also paid special attention to the viability of a start-up. Looking into things as early adopter interviews and usability tests.
